All about me

I am a third-year Interaction Design student at PES University, Bangalore, with a strong passion for creating intuitive and user-centred designs. My interests lie in graphic design and branding, and I have gained diverse experience through internships, including one in interior design and two in graphic design. Currently, I am working as a graphic design intern, where I get to hone my creative skills and learn various marketing strategies.

In addition to design, I enjoy sketching, reading novels, and unwinding with Netflix. I have a fascination with criminal psychology, often immersing myself in studies about the minds of criminals and the psychology behind their actions. As an introvert, I consider myself a keen observer, always taking in details that help inform my designs and understanding of the world.
